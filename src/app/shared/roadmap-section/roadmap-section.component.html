Roadmap

Dive into the Soline journey, where we transform Twitter engagement into tangible rewards. From concept to full-scale deployment, our roadmap is your guide to our ambitious milestones. Stay tuned for pivotal updates as we advance towards a decentralized digital landscape.

Q3 2023 Ideation and Conceptualization
  • Develop the Soline project concept.
  • Market research and analysis.
  • Initial whitepaper draft.
Q4 2023 Team Assembly and Whitepaper Finalization
  • Assemble a core team of developers, marketers, and strategists.
  • Finalize the Soline whitepaper.
  • Establish partnerships with key stakeholders.
Q1 2024 Platform Development and Testnet Launch
  • Begin platform development.
  • Launch the Soline testnet.
  • Conduct initial security audits.
Q2 2024 Pre-Sale and Community Building
  • Conduct token pre-sale.
  • Expand community engagement.
  • Refine platform based on testnet feedback.
Q3 2024 Public Sale and Beta Release
  • Conduct public sale of SLN tokens.
  • Release Soline beta for public use.
  • Integrate initial set of APIs with Twitter.
Q4 2024 Full Platform Launch
  • Officially launch the full Soline platform.
  • Activate all core features like the decentralized ad exchange.
  • Begin onboarding content creators and advertisers.
Q1 2025 Expansion of Services
  • Scale up the decentralized ad exchange.
  • Introduce advanced analytics tools.
  • Expand NFT marketplace functionalities.
Q2 2025 Ecosystem Maturation
  • Optimize tokenomics based on platform usage.
  • Deepen engagement with Twitter Spaces.
  • Enhance AI features for personalized content and ad targeting.
Q3 2025 and Beyond Further Innovation and Growth
  • Continuous platform improvement.
  • Expand enterprise-level services.
  • Explore additional social media integrations.
